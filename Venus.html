Structure

This planet has an iron core, a mantle made of hot rock and a thin crust of rock.

Surface

This planet has mountains, valleys, and tens of thousands of volcanoes. The landscape is dusty and covered in craters.

Atmosphere

he atmosphere of this planet consists mainly of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid droplets. The thick atmosphere traps the Sun's heat, and the planet is covered with clouds that reflect and scatter sunlight. Atmospheric lightning bursts often occur in the quick-moving clouds closer to the surface.

Length of Day

243 Earth days
