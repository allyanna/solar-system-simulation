Surface

The surface of the Sun, the photosphere, region, is not a solid surface like the surfaces of planets. Instead, this is the outer layer of the gassy star were most of the stars radiation escapes.

Atmosphere

Above the photosphere lie the tenuous chromosphere and the corona, which make up the thin solar atmosphere.
