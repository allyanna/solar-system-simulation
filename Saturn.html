Saturn

Structure

This planet is made mostly of hydrogen and helium. At it’s center is a dense core of metals like iron and nickel surrounded by rocky material and other compounds solidified by the intense pressure and heat. It is enveloped by liquid metallic hydrogen inside a layer of liquid

Surface

As a gas giant, this planet doesn’t have a true surface. The planet is mostly swirling gases and liquids. Deep in the atmosphere, pressure and temperature increase, compressing the hydrogen gas into a liquid giving it an ocean made of hydrogen.

Atmosphere

This planet is is blanketed with clouds that appear as faint stripes, jet streams and storms.The north pole has an interesting atmospheric feature—a six-sided jet stream. There is no weather feature like it anywhere else in the solar system.
