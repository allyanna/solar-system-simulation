Mercury

Structure

This Planet has a large metallic core and there is evidence that it is partly molten, or liquid. It also has a thin outer shell.

Surface

The surface of this planet is scarred by many impact craters resulting from collisions with meteoroids and comets. Most of this planet's surface would appear greyish-brown to the human eye.Temperatures on the surface are extreme, both hot and cold. This planet may have water ice at its north and south poles inside deep craters,in regions of permanent shadow.

Atmosphere

This planet has a thin exosphere made up of atoms blasted off the surface by the solar wind and striking meteoroids. The exosphere is composed mostly of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium and potassium.
